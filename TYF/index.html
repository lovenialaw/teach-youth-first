Who We Are

Teach Youth First is dedicated to providing quality education and support to underprivileged children in Sarawak. Our comprehensive approach includes:

📚

Weekly Resources

We provide weekly educational materials and resources to support continuous learning and development.

🎨

Learning Supplies

Essential supplies including books, stationery, and educational tools are distributed to ensure effective learning.

👶

Age-Based Learning

Tailored educational programs designed for different age groups, ensuring appropriate development at each stage.

👥

Community Support

Regular community engagement and parent education programs to create a supportive learning environment.
