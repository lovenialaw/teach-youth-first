OUR STORY

Empowering children through education and community support.

Who Are We

Teach Youth First (TYF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging educational gaps for children in Sarawak, Malaysia. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our team of passionate volunteers works tirelessly to provide English and Mathematics lessons to children in various communities, including children's homes and schools across Sarawak. Beyond that, we also conduct skills development workshops for primary school students.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education to children in need, fostering their academic growth and personal development through dedicated teaching and community support.

Our Vision

A Sarawak where every child has equal access to quality education, enabling them to reach their full potential and contribute positively to their communities.

OUR PARTNERS

Working together with trusted organizations to create lasting impact in children's education.

The Salvation Army

Children's Home

The Salvation Army Kuching Children's Care Centre is a children's foundation located in Kota Samarahan, Sarawak. We teach them introductory English lessons biweekly on Saturday.

20+ Children Supported

Rumah Kanak-Kanak

Children's Home

Rumah Kanak-kanak Kuching is a children's foundation located at Siburan, Sarawak. Children who reside are mostly younger, in the Kindergarten to lower Primary levels.

30+ Children Supported

Pertubuhan Kebajikan Baiturrahmah

Children's Home

Pertubuhan Kebajikan Baiturrahmah Kuching is a children's foundation located at Petra Jaya, Kuching. We teach them Mathematics biweekly on Saturday.

10+ Children Supported

Yayasan Lasallian Kuching

Non-Profit Organization

Yayasan Lasallian Kuching advances education amongst community outreach in Sarawak. We conduct biweekly skills development workshops for primary school students across Sarawak.
