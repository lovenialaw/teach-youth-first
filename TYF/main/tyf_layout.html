Aiming to make early education accessible for underprivileged children in Sarawak.

Explore programs

Weekly resources

We produce weekly English, Maths and Environmental resources with fun activities to help motivate and encourage young children, which are dropped off at respective foundations every week.

Supplies

In addition to our booklets, supplies such as laptops, projectors, notebooks and pens are provided to our partners.

Age-based learning

Our programs and resources are curated for children in Primary levels.

Our mission is to foster the learning journeys of underprivileged children in Indonesia, through study packets for various subjects.

Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.

Malcolm X

Workshops

Our bi-monthly workshops are an opportunity for children to embrace a new challenge. Through online and in-person workshops, children will engage in creative activities such as yoga and arts and craft.

Building motivation and work ethic

Our programs encourage children to become better independent learners; weve also included opportunities for them to try their hardest and compete with one another to earn a prize at the end of every month through a test encapsulating that months content. This will motivate them and build a work ethic they can take away in their lives.
